# Discrete Transforms 

## Discrete Fourier Transform (DFT) 
$$ \Large X_{n} = \sum_{k=0}^{N-1} x_{k} e^{-j \frac{2\pi}{N} n k} $$

## Inverse DFT
$$ \Large x_{k} = \dfrac{1}{N} \sum_{n=0}^{N-1} X_{n} e^{j \frac{2\pi}{N} k n} $$
